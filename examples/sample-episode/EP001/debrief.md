## Lessons for next time

- Cut Earnings Watch to 6 minutes. The format is most useful as a punchy beat, not a deep-dive. Save deep-dives for full topic segments.
- Open the LinkedIn post with the punchiest line. Lead with the contrarian take, not the news framing.
- Build a "payoff-line check" into Segment 2 when running long — a planned closing line that lets the host land the segment cleanly even when time is short.

## Topic-radar signal

Over-weight: programmatic infrastructure stories (the Spotify framing carried). Quietly-priced product moves at frontier labs (the Sonnet read landed).

Under-weight: same-week pricing reactions (the OpenAI Plus cut underperformed as the third item — too much overlap with the Anthropic segment).

Avoid covering for at least four weeks: Anthropic, Spotify, OpenAI Plus pricing. Listeners will read repetition as the show running thin.
