# EP001 — Co-host Brief — Anthropic's Opus 5 and the Spotify ad miracle

Target runtime: 45 min. Recording: 2026-05-04. Question density: standard (3 per segment).

## Cold open

Throw to host:
- "Two big moves this week — Anthropic shipped Opus 5 and Spotify reported. Where do you want to start?"

## Pacing principles

- Let the host run for 90 seconds before throwing the first follow-up. The opening monologue is where the take lands.
- If the host repeats a number twice, you can skip the next planned question. They've already covered the ground.
- Push back once per segment, even if you agree. The contrarian beat carries the show.
- Save the personal anecdote questions for late in a segment. Early questions should hand the host data, not memory.

## Segment 1: Anthropic Opus 5 launch (14 min)

Throw line:
- "Anthropic dropped Opus 5 on Tuesday. First impressions?"

Follow-up questions:
- "If Sonnet is the volume play, why announce Opus 5 first? Optics or strategy?"
- "What's the read on the pricing held flat — confidence or constraint?"
- "Is there a world where the third-place lab wins on price and capability simultaneously?"

Articles to have queued:
- [Anthropic Opus 5 announcement](https://example.com/anthropic-opus-5-bench) — official launch post with the LongBench-2 numbers.
- [Dario interview](https://example.com/dario-interview) — the "models becoming commodities" line.
- [The Information on Anthropic enterprise revenue](https://example.com/the-information-anthropic) — the 60/40 API/Claude.ai split.

Cue: If the host hasn't mentioned Sonnet 5 by minute 5, throw "What about the Sonnet variant they shipped alongside?"

## Earnings Watch (8 min)

Throw line:
- "Spotify reported Thursday. Stock popped. What did you see in the call?"

Follow-up questions:
- "If MAU is slowing, how long does monetization carry the stock?"
- "Apple's audio ad business — are they about to copy this playbook?"

Articles to have queued:
- [Spotify Q1 release](https://example.com/spotify-q1-2026-earnings) — the headline numbers.
- [Spotify developer update](https://example.com/spotify-developer-update) — the 4.2B API requests number is the buried lede.

Cue: If the host frames this as "podcast ads are back," interject with "But ad load doubled — is this really a podcast story?"

## Segment 2: OpenAI cuts Plus pricing 20% (10 min)

Throw line:
- "OpenAI cut Plus pricing 20% on Friday. Reaction or strategy?"

Follow-up questions:
- "Will the consumer price hold or is this the floor for the next 12 months?"
- "Does this hit the IPO narrative?"
- "Is this churn defense or growth offense?"

Articles to have queued:
- [The Information on OpenAI Plus pricing](https://example.com/openai-plus-cut) — official pricing change.
- [Plus subscriber leak from Q4](https://example.com/openai-leaked-data) — the 18M subscriber estimate.

Cue: If the host pivots to AGI talk, gently steer back with "but on the dollars — does $80M/month off the topline matter?"

## Closing

Sign-off cue: After the host's specific ask, hand back with "I'll see you next week."
